## Description
Music Player is a focused, offline music player for the songs you keep on your device.

Import audio files straight from the Files app and play them instantly — no account, no streaming, no ads, no tracking. Everything stays on your iPhone.

FEATURES
• Import your own audio (MP3, M4A, AAC, AIFF, WAV and more)
• Gapless background playback — keep listening with the screen off
• Lock screen and Control Center controls
• Shuffle and repeat (off / all / one)
• Search and sort your library by date or title
• A premium, dark, animated now-playing experience
• 100% offline. Your library never leaves your device.

No sign-up. No subscription. Just your music.
