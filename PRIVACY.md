# Privacy Policy — Music Player

_Last updated: 2026-06-25_

Music Player ("the app") is an offline music player. Your privacy is simple to describe
because the app is built to need nothing from you.

## Data we collect
**None.** The app does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.

- The app has no account or sign-in.
- The app makes no network requests. Nothing leaves your device.
- The audio files you import and your library list are stored only locally on your
  device, in the app's private storage. We never see them.
- We use no analytics, no advertising, no third-party SDKs, and no tracking.

## Permissions
The app plays audio in the background (so playback continues with the screen locked).
It does not request access to the microphone, contacts, location, photos, or any other
personal data.

## Children
The app collects no data from anyone, including children.
